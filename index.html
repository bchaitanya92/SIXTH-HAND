About

Who we are

At sixth hand, we are dedicated to empowering visually impaired students by breaking down barriers to learning. Our mission is to create an inclusive educational experience by leveraging innovative technology that transforms the way students interact with text and images. We provide tools that convert written content into speech, allowing learners to hear and understand materials effortlessly. Additionally, our platform turns images into descriptive audio narratives, making visual information accessible to all. Our commitment is rooted in fostering independence, enhancing education, and opening up opportunities for every student to thrive. Together, we’re redefining the possibilities of accessible learning.
